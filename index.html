How It Works

Your investigation workflow, streamlined.

Violet follows the same process you already use — it just handles the writing so you can focus on the thinking.

1

Enter your intake notes

Paste or type your intake notes from the HRBP referral. Violet generates a case synopsis and suggests scope questions.

2

Generate your investigation plan

Violet identifies the allegations, suggests witnesses and documents to review, and drafts your scope of investigation. You review and adjust.

3

Conduct interviews with tailored outlines

For each party, Violet generates a custom outline based on the case evidence — using funnel methodology and confrontation sequencing. Enter your notes after each interview.

4

Set your finding and generate the report

You decide the finding direction. Violet drafts the full report — facts, credibility analysis, and finding — sourced entirely from your interview notes and exhibits. Review it, edit it, make it yours.

5

Export and close

Send the report to Google Docs, record the resolution, and close the case. Everything is tracked and stored.
