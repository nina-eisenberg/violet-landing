How It Works

The full compliance lifecycle, streamlined.

Violet covers every stage — from setting up your policies to closing the case and training your team.

1

Build your policies

Draft workplace policies tailored to your jurisdiction and industry, or upload your existing ones. Violet analyzes them for gaps and fixes issues with one click. Then generate an employee handbook and track acknowledgments.

2

An employee tells Violet

Someone has a concern. They chat with Violet on the web, talk in their browser, or call the hotline. Violet listens, asks clarifying questions, and submits a report to your inbox.

3

Violet drafts your investigation

Generate an investigation plan, tailored interview outlines that build on prior testimony, and a full report with credibility analysis and policy citations.

4

You set the finding

Review the AI draft, set your findings and recommendations. Violet warns you if the evidence doesn't support your conclusion. Export to Google Docs and close the case.

5

Train and prevent

Generate ethics training from your closed cases. Employees review the policy, pass a quick check, then practice handling the scenario in a voice conversation with Violet — and get scored. Assign to your whole team, track completion, and identify where education is needed most.
