Built for Investigators

Every step of the investigation, accelerated.

From intake to close-out, Violet generates professional artifacts while you stay focused on the evidence and the people.

Tell Violet

Employees report concerns by chatting with Violet on the web or calling a dedicated phone number. Violet has a natural conversation, asks smart follow-up questions, and submits a structured report to your inbox — 24/7, in 50+ languages. No forms. No hold music. No scripts.

AI-Powered Inbox

Every report lands in a triage queue with AI-generated summaries, urgency ratings, and suggested categories. Review the full conversation, send anonymous follow-up questions to the reporter, or convert to a full investigation case with one click.

Investigation Toolkit

Violet generates investigation plans, interview outlines tailored to each witness with funnel methodology and confrontation sequencing, and full investigation reports with credibility analysis — all sourced from your interview notes and exhibits.

Investigator in Control

AI drafts, you decide. Violet writes the analysis but the investigator sets the findings and recommendations. Every report is reviewed and edited before it goes anywhere. Legally defensible by design.

A real phone number. An AI that listens.

Give your employees a dedicated hotline number. When they call, Violet answers — has a natural conversation, captures the details, and drops a fully triaged report in your inbox. What traditional hotlines charge $10-50K/year for, Violet does for pennies per call.

How It Works

Your investigation workflow, streamlined.

Violet follows the same process you already use — it just handles the writing so you can focus on the thinking.

1

An employee tells Violet

Someone has a concern. They chat with Violet on the web or call the hotline. Violet listens, asks a few clarifying questions, and submits a report.

2

You triage in the Inbox

The report lands in your Inbox with an AI summary, urgency rating, and suggested category. Convert to a case with one click.

3

Violet drafts your investigation

Generate an investigation plan, tailored interview outlines for each party, and a full investigation report with credibility analysis.

4

You set the finding

Review the AI draft, edit it, set your findings and recommendations. Export to Google Docs and close the case.
